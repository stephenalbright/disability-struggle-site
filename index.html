Our story

Why this exists

In 2020, Stephen Albright went in for brain surgery and woke up with aphasia. The words were still in his head — he just couldn't get them out. Saying his own name, reading a menu, finishing a sentence: all of it had to be relearned, one rep at a time.

The hardest part wasn't the weekly therapy hour. It was the other six days — alone at the kitchen table, no one to practice with, no way to tell if he was getting better. So he built the thing he'd needed: AphasiaGPT, a companion that lets people practice every day, between sessions, at their own pace.

Disability Driven is that idea widened to every brain that has to relearn how to connect — built by someone who has done the relearning.

Nothing about us, without us.

Stephen Albright — Founder, Disability Driven
